Who We Are

   
     

        LedgerLink Consulting is a client-first financial consulting firm based in Kolkata, India. With a passion for numbers and a commitment to trust, our mission is to provide businesses with accurate, timely, and insightful financial solutions tailored to their needs.      

     

        Founded by Argha Kumar Mondal and Subhajit Ghosh, LedgerLink was built with the belief that every business deserves professional-grade accounting and strategic support — regardless of size or industry. With deep expertise in finance, compliance, and human resources, our team delivers practical solutions with lasting impact.      

     

        Whether you're a startup, a growing business, or an established enterprise, LedgerLink is here to be your trusted partner. Our work is guided by our values: accuracy, integrity, transparency, and long-term client relationships.
